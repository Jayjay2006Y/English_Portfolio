Summer Updates

Highlights, a lowlight, and pictures.

Highlights

The 3 highlights were seeing my family in the US. I usually visit them once or twice a year, but it’s still always nice to see them again. The beaches in California were nice and sunny. It was great for a morning run and to swim. England and Ireland were also nice to visit and to see tourist spots like St Paul’s Cathedral or the Guinness brewery in Dublin.

Lowlight

The lowlight was that I had a long layover because I flew from Zurich to Atlanta and had a connecting flight to Chicago O’Hare. First the flight was delayed by 4 hours. Then when we got on the flight and left the gate, we had to go back due to a medical emergency, and we stayed even longer because of bad weather.
