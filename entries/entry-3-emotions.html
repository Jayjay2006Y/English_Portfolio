Emotions

Emotion vocabulary with example sentences.

  • Desperate – I was desperate to get my driver’s permit because I wanted to learn how to drive.
  • Disappointed – Once I heard that the Chicago Blackhawks sold Patrick Kane to the Red Wings, I was disappointed.
  • Delighted – I was delighted to hear that I would be able to start in an interesting team in my firm.
  • Devastated – I was devastated when Barcelona got kicked out of the Champions League by Inter Milan.
  • Astonished – When I saw St Paul’s Cathedral, I was astonished by how tall it was.
