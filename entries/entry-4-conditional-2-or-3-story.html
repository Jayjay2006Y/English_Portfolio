Conditional 2 or 3 Story

A “what if” story using conditional grammar.

If I hadn’t started my apprenticeship, I wouldn’t have known what to do. Maybe I would have gone to private school, or maybe I would have waited until the military and started a career there. I just knew I wanted to join, because I wasn’t fascinated by the other jobs.
