Best of 2025

Music opinions + personal highlights.

1. Albums from Pitchfork’s list I listened to and my thoughts

I listened to Playboi Carti’s album I AM MUSIC. I thought it was overrated. Everyone waited five years for a 30-song album, but I feel like only about five songs were really good. Most of the tracks had the same sound and buildup, so it started to feel repetitive.

2. Which albums from the list I listened to and which I liked most

There were a couple of albums where I had only heard one song on the radio. From Playboi Carti’s album, I had heard a few songs, but besides that, most of the albums I listen to weren’t on this list because they are older. I didn’t know many of the albums on Pitchfork’s list very well, so out of the ones I heard, I’d say Playboi Carti’s album was my favorite. I’m just not a big fan of most of the other artists on the list.

3. Albums from 2025 that should be on the list

I think Jackboys 2 by Travis Scott and Cactus Jack should have been on the list. It had several songs I liked, and it featured other artists like Don Toliver, whose music I also enjoy. I’m a big fan of Travis Scott and Don Toliver, and I’ve even been to concerts for both of them, so this album stood out to me.

4. Some of the BEST things of 2025 for me

  1. Going to the Don Toliver concert.
  2. Seeing Inter Miami win the MLS Cup — I’m a big Messi fan.
  3. Getting to work in my desired IT field, especially on a team focused on security.
  4. My job agreeing to pay for my cyber security certification.
  5. Going to the Switzerland vs. Czech Republic hockey game with my friends — it was a fun experience even though Switzerland lost.
